history

The history of Telaga Putri began during the Mataram kingdom, when the Kaliurang area was still a royal territory.
That said, Nyai Ontrowulan often visited this lake to relax and enjoy its natural beauty. The lake is named Telaga Putri in honor of its existence. This lake water is believed to have good properties for health and beauty.
During the Dutch colonial period, Kaliurang became a resting place for colonial officials. They are attracted by the natural beauty and cool climate of the area. Telaga Putri is one of the popular places to visit.
